Morgan Thinks She Saw UFO at Disney - The Bobby Bones Show

Published 2025-08-21 13:32 UTC

UFO Sighted at Disney by Local Woman

Morgan has reported seeing a UFO while at Disney's EPCOT on Saturday evening. She was sitting on a patio with her boyfriend, waiting for the fireworks show, when they noticed a bright light in the sky.

This sighting has drawn interest as it adds to the ongoing discussions about unidentified flying objects. Local authorities have not been involved in the incident, and no further investigations have been reported at this time.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on Saturday evening at EPCOT, Disney.
  • The bright light was observed for several minutes.
  • Morgan recorded a video of the light but did not capture its disappearance.
  • Disney does not use drones during fireworks shows at EPCOT.
  • Other patrons on the patio also witnessed the light.

Timeline

On Saturday evening, Morgan and her boyfriend were seated on a patio at EPCOT, waiting for the fireworks show to start. They spotted a very bright light in the sky that appeared suddenly and remained stationary for several minutes. After the light disappeared, there was noticeable excitement among other patrons on the patio.

Morgan attempted to identify the light and ruled out both a drone and a satellite. She recorded a video of the event but could not capture the moment the light vanished.

Official statements

"I thought it was definitely a UFO with aliens," Morgan said, reflecting on her experience. "The guys were laughing at me, saying it wasn't aliens."

Context

This sighting adds to a growing number of reported UFO encounters, which have gained attention in various media outlets. While many such claims remain unverified, they continue to spark curiosity and debate among enthusiasts and skeptics alike.

What’s next

No official investigation is underway regarding this sighting, and Disney has not commented on the incident. Morgan's experience remains anecdotal, with no additional evidence or follow-up reported.
