Witness Claims UFO Sighting at Disney's EPCOT

Morgan has reported seeing a UFO while at Disney's EPCOT in Florida on Saturday evening. She was sitting on a patio with her boyfriend, waiting for the fireworks show, when they noticed a bright light in the sky. The light reportedly appeared suddenly, was stationary, and then vanished after several minutes.

This incident has gained attention as it raises questions about unidentified aerial phenomena and the public's interest in UFO sightings. Morgan has shared her experience and video evidence, although some observers have expressed skepticism about her claims.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on a Saturday evening at EPCOT, Disney World, Florida.
  • Morgan and her boyfriend were waiting for the fireworks show when they noticed the light.
  • The light was described as very bright and stationary for several minutes.
  • Other patrons on the patio also witnessed the light.
  • Morgan recorded a video of the light, but did not capture its disappearance.
  • Disney has no drones involved in fireworks shows, according to Morgan's research.

Timeline

Morgan and her boyfriend arrived at EPCOT to enjoy the evening fireworks. While seated on the patio, they spotted a bright light in the sky that appeared suddenly. After several minutes of observing the light, it vanished without any movement. Morgan recorded a video of the event but did not capture the moment the light disappeared. Following the sighting, she researched the possibility of the light being a drone, only to find that Disney does not use drones during the fireworks show.

Official statements

"It was super bright, not moving, and then just blipped away," Morgan said about her sighting.
"I thought it could be a drone, but Disney doesn’t have any drones involved in the fireworks show," she added.

Context

UFO sightings have become a topic of increasing interest in recent years, with many individuals reporting similar experiences. The phenomenon has prompted discussions among enthusiasts and skeptics alike. Public interest often spikes when sightings occur in popular locations, such as theme parks.

What’s next

There are currently no official investigations into the sighting at Disney's EPCOT, and Morgan has not disclosed any plans to pursue further inquiries.
