Otherwise, we risk letting history repeat itself, allowing the powerful to escape unscathed while the victims are left in the dust.

In the end, if Maxwell receives a pardon, it wouldn’t just be a “huge error”—it would be a catastrophic failure of our moral compass. So let’s raise our voices, demand transparency, and ensure that those who exploit others face the consequences of their actions. Because if we don’t, we might as well throw in the towel and declare that justice is dead.
