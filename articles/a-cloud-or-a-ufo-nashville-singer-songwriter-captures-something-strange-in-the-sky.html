A Cloud Or A UFO? Nashville Singer-Songwriter Captures Something Strange in the Sky

Whitney Merritt, an aspiring singer-songwriter from Owensboro, Kentucky, has reported a peculiar sighting in the sky while walking in her new neighborhood in White House, Tennessee, on Labor Day, September 4, 2023. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has sparked discussions among local residents and social media users about unidentified flying objects and meteorological phenomena. Authorities have not confirmed any investigations related to the incident as of now.

Key facts

  • Incident date: September 4, 2023
  • Location: White House, Tennessee
  • Duration of sighting: At least 10 minutes
  • Witness: Whitney Merritt
  • Object identified as: Lenticular cloud

Timeline

Whitney Merritt was walking in her neighborhood on September 4 when she noticed an unusual object in the sky. She described the area as having "creepy suburbia vibes" before turning onto a street and spotting the object hovering in the sky.

After observing it for around ten minutes, she decided to document the sighting with her phone and shared the video on social media. While some friends suggested it was a UFO, others identified it as a lenticular cloud.

Official statements

“What in the HECKKKKK?”
“The more I looked at it, it reminded me of the melted clock in the Salvador Dali artwork. Kinda.”

Context

Lenticular clouds are known for their unique, saucer-like shape and can often be mistaken for UFOs. This phenomenon has been reported in various locations, leading to public interest and speculation about unidentified aerial objects.

What’s next

As of now, no formal investigations have been initiated regarding this sighting. Whitney Merritt continues to share her experience on social media while discussions about the phenomenon remain active among her followers.
