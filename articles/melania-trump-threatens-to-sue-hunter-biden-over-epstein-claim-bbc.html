Melania Trump Threatens Legal Action Against Hunter Biden Over Epstein Claims

In a jaw-dropping turn of events that has the political world buzzing, Melania Trump has decided to take a stand against Hunter Biden, the son of President Joe Biden. According to reports from the BBC, Melania is not taking these allegations lightly. The source of her ire? A claim made by Hunter Biden that insinuates a connection between the Trump family and Epstein, the infamous financier who was charged with sex trafficking minors. The audacity! Hunter Biden's comments have reportedly sparked outrage, leading Melania to contemplate a lawsuit to protect her family's reputation.

"The claims made by Hunter Biden are completely unfounded and damaging," a source close to Melania stated. "She is considering all legal options to defend her name and that of her family."
